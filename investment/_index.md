Investment covers the structural architecture of direct-hold real estate as an asset
class. Articles in this category describe the principles of direct beneficial ownership,
the four vehicle types used to deliver direct-hold solutions across jurisdictions, the
perpetual equity model, and the investor eligibility framework. Content is structural and
conceptual — how direct-hold investment works as an architecture, not the terms of any
specific offering.

Direct-hold investment prioritizes full beneficial ownership of underlying assets and
eliminates the asset-sale-required-for-exit constraint inherent in pooled fund structures.

Woodfine Management Corp. structures Direct-Hold Solutions intended to extend
the financial and operational longevity of commercial assets through per-asset
legal isolation, no-redemption equity, and a 1.2x interest-coverage floor. This
encyclopedia is the corporate reference for those five frameworks — covering
direct-hold structure, equity transfer, fiduciary data mandate, interest coverage,
and redemption elimination.

Forward-looking statements in this wiki reflect current intentions and are
subject to risk and uncertainty; statements are framed using planned, intended,
or target language per the firm's continuous-disclosure practice under NI 51-102
and OSC SN 51-721.
